5 Tools I Can't Live Without

Optimizing the workflow for speed and sanity.

A craftsman is only as good as their tools. Over the years, I've curated a setup that minimizes friction and maximizes flow state.

  1. VS Code + Extensions: Prettier, ESLint, and GitLens are non-negotiable.
  2. Docker: "It works on my machine" is solved. Consistent environments everywhere.
  3. Postman: Essential for API development and testing endpoints before frontend integration.
  4. Notion: My second brain. Project tracking, documentation, and life organization.
  5. Figma: Even as a dev, I need to visualize before I build.

What's in your stack? Always be open to trying new workflows that might save you those precious extra minutes.
